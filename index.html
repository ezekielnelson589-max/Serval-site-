Transparency & Verification (FAQ)

Q: Why is the 17-digit Chassis Number mandatory for all orders?

A: Automotive manufacturers change internal mechatronics and electrical layouts even within the exact same production year. Locking down the 17-digit vehicle identification code allows us to cross-reference directly with international database networks, guaranteeing 100% part accuracy and eliminating expensive return freight delays.

Q: How do you avoid middleman retail pricing structures?

A: Traditional dealers purchase from multiple layers of domestic distributors. Serval Dynamics functions as an independent direct-to-factory router. We buy parts directly from manufacturing platforms in China, Europe, and the US, bypassing local price inflation.

Q: What is the operational duration for logistics delivery?

A: We operate two primary routes. Our Express Air Cargo pipeline takes 5 to 7 business days, ideal for urgent mechatronics modules and control blocks. Bulk sea logistics channels are used for volume stock and heavy mechanical hardware arrays.

Q: Do you offer on-site technical diagnostics across fleets?

A: Yes. For corporate clients and complex diagnostics, we combine advanced digital tracking systems and on-site vehicle verification straight at your operational hub, preventing high towing costs and minimizing total vehicle downtime.
